Dr. Kaynat Mozammil

Welcome to Jamia Millia Islamia, where we strive to provide an environment that fosters growth, knowledge, and innovation. Our commitment is to nurture future leaders who can tackle real-world challenges. Together, we aim for excellence in education and research.

At Jamia Millia Islamia, we believe in a holistic approach to education, combining academics with co-curricular activities to prepare our students for a fulfilling career and life. We look forward to your journey with us.

Vision and Mission

Our vision is to create a transformative educational experience for students, focusing on deep disciplinary knowledge, problem-solving skills, leadership development, and fostering a community of innovation. Our mission is to provide high-quality education to empower students to succeed in a rapidly evolving world.

Biography

Dr. Kaynat Mozammil has been leading Jamia Millia Islamia as principal since 2022. With over 14 years of experience in academic leadership and a doctorate, Dr. Kaynat has worked extensively in higher education reform and global academic cooperation. His passion for education has driven various initiatives aimed at fostering student development, community engagement, and institutional growth.
